Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Eggs 1
Sugar 2 spoons
Butter 20 g
Baking powder 1 teaspoon

Steps:

Step 1: Mix flour, sugar and baking powder.
Step 2: Add milk and egg, mix well.
Step 3: Heat a pan and add butter.
Step 4: Pour the batter into the pan.
Step 5: Cook both sides until golden.

Caesar Salad

  • Time : 15 minutes
  • Serves : 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 200 g
Lettuce 1 bunch
Croutons 1 cup
Parmesan cheese 50 g
Caesar sauce 3 spoons

Steps:

Step 1: Cut and fry the chicken.
Step 2: Wash and cut the lettuce.
Step 3: Put lettuce in a bowl.
Step 4: Add chicken and croutons.
Step 5: Add sauce and cheese and mix well.

Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cup
Milk 1 cup
Eggs 3
Sugar 1 cup
Butter 100 g
Baking powder 1 teaspoon
Cocoa powder ½ cup

Steps:

Step 1: Mix sugar and eggs.
Step 2:Add milk and melted butter.
Step 3: Add flour, cocoa powder and baking powder.
Step 4: Mix well until smooth.
Step 5: Pour into a baking form.
Step 6: Bake for 35–40 minutes.
